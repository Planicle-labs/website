# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Startup founders and early-stage teams (Seed to Series B) who need a high-quality digital presence to establish credibility, attract investors, and acquire users. They move fast, value execution over talk, and need a technical partner who ships premium work without hand-holding.

## Product Purpose
Planicle is a premium software agency that builds websites, apps, and AI workflows. The purpose of this landing page is to immediately instill confidence, showcase technical excellence, and convince startup founders to book a deal.

## Brand Personality
Premium, confident, precise.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS templates, cheap "Fiverr-style" agency pages, overly playful or cartoonish aesthetics, cluttered or messy layouts.

## Design Principles
- **Premium restraint:** Avoid generic startup tropes; use high-quality typography and a sleek, high-contrast dark aesthetic that feels expensive.
- **Exceptional Motion:** Implement a simple, smoothly and minimally animated style. Use buttery-smooth microinteractions that scream quality without being excessive. Balance is key—rely on exponential ease-out curves and subtle shifts rather than bouncy or hyperactive movement.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Modern web standards (WCAG 2.1 AA baseline). Semantic HTML, proper contrast, and considerate motion.
